The last Kingdom

"The Last Kingdom" is a historical drama series set in 9th and 10th century England, depicting the clash between Saxons and Vikings. It follows the story of Uhtred of Bebbanburg, a Saxon nobleman captured and raised by Vikings, who must navigate his divided loyalties and reclaim his ancestral home.

Game Of Thrones GOT

"Game of Thrones" is a fantasy drama series based on George R.R. Martin's novels, set in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos. It follows the power struggles among noble families as they vie for control of the Iron Throne and face threats from ancient, supernatural forces.

7 Kings Must Die

"7 Kings Must Die" is a historical drama film that continues the story from "The Last Kingdom," focusing on the turbulent final years of Viking-era England. It follows the aftermath of Uhtred of Bebbanburg’s epic journey as he confronts new challenges and power struggles in a fractured kingdom.

The Northman

The Northman is a gritty Viking epic following Prince Amleth’s quest for vengeance against his uncle, who murdered his father. With stunning visuals and relentless action, it’s a gripping tale of betrayal and redemption set in a brutal, mythic world.

Barbarians

Barbarian is an epic historical drama that chronicles the rise of a rebellious warrior as he unites ancient tribes against the might of the Roman Empire. The series vividly brings to life the clash of cultures and the brutal realities of warfare in a tumultuous era.

House of the Dragon

House of the Dragon delves into the fiery history of House Targaryen, exploring the power struggles and dragon-fueled drama that precede the events of Game of Thrones. Set against a backdrop of political intrigue and epic battles, the series unveils the origins of the Targaryen dynasty's tumultuous rise and fall.
